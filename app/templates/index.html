In the Eighties, he was a heart-throb. In the Nineties, he was a staple(?). By the aughts(?) he was the subject a drinking game. Or, if you prefer your games more genteel, a "parlor" game.

Six Degrees of Kevin Bacon was a play on the concept of six degrees of separation. Any two people in the world could be linked by six personal connections... or something like that.

The game is to begin with some actor, any actor (or actress) and try to link them, through films they have worked on, with Kevin Bacon within six degrees of separation.

If we start with Brad Pitt. And Brad Pitt had worked with Kevin Bacon on some film. That's one degree of separation. And if you make that link, you win. It is that easy! '

But if Brad hadn't worked with Kevin but he HAD worked with Morgan Freeman who HAD worked with Kevin. That's two degrees of separation. And, if you make that link, within six degrees... then you win.
